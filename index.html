How to Play

  • Tap to Start: The timer begins counting up
  • Tap to Stop: Freeze the timer at your chosen moment
  • Hit the Target: Your time must match the target exactly (to 2 decimal places)
  • Perfect Hit: Advance to the next level with confetti and celebration
  • Miss: Lose a life and try again
  • Complete 10 Levels: Unlock new worlds and challenges

Frequently Asked Questions

How many lives do I get?
You start with 25 lives per day. Lives reset at midnight UTC. You can watch optional rewarded ads to earn additional lives if you run out.
What does "2 decimal precision" mean?
Your timer must land exactly on the target time to two decimal places. For example, if the target is 3.00 seconds, stopping at 2.99 or 3.01 will count as a miss. The timer rounds to 2 decimal places for evaluation.
Is the game free?
Yes! The core game is completely free to play. There are optional rewarded ads you can watch to earn extra lives, but there are no paywalls blocking your progress.
When do lives reset?
Lives reset daily at 00:00 UTC (midnight UTC). This ensures fair gameplay across all time zones.
How do I unlock new worlds?
Complete 10 levels in the current world to unlock the next one. Each world introduces new themes and challenges.
My timer seems laggy or unresponsive. What should I do?
Try closing other apps running in the background and ensure your device has enough free memory. If the issue persists, try restarting your device. Contact support if problems continue.
Can I play offline?
The core gameplay works offline, but you'll need an internet connection for progress syncing, watching rewarded ads, and accessing certain features.
How is my data used?
Stop It! uses anonymous Firebase authentication to save your progress. We collect minimal data necessary for gameplay (level progress, lives, settings) and advertising. See our Privacy Policy below for full details.
Can I disable ads?
All ads in the game are optional rewarded ads that you choose to watch in exchange for extra lives. There are no forced interstitial ads interrupting your gameplay.
Will my progress sync across devices?
Yes! Your progress is saved to the cloud using Firebase, so you can pick up where you left off on any device.

Privacy & Data

What data we collect:

  • Anonymous user ID for progress tracking
  • Game progress (levels completed, current world, lives remaining)
  • Device advertising identifier (for relevant ads, with your consent)
  • Basic analytics (crashes, performance metrics)

What we DON'T collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Any data not directly related to gameplay

Third-party services:

  • Firebase: Cloud storage for game progress and analytics
  • AdMob: Serves optional rewarded ads

We respect your privacy. All data collection complies with GDPR, CCPA, and Apple's App Tracking Transparency requirements.

System Requirements

  • Platform: iOS 13.0 or later
  • Devices: iPhone only (iPad support coming soon)
  • Storage: ~50 MB
  • Internet: Optional (required for progress sync and ads)

Troubleshooting

Game won't launch or crashes on startup

  • Ensure you're running iOS 13.0 or later
  • Restart your device
  • Delete and reinstall the app
  • Ensure you have at least 100 MB of free storage

Progress not saving

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure the app has permission to access the network
  • Try signing out and back in (if applicable)

Timer feels inaccurate

  • The timer updates at 60 FPS for maximum precision
  • Ensure no other apps are causing performance issues
  • Close background apps to free up device resources
