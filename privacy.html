Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Stop It! ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Stop It! does NOT require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the app:

  • Anonymous User ID: A randomly generated identifier to save your game progress
  • Game Progress Data: Levels completed, current world, lives remaining, and settings
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage Analytics: App crashes, performance metrics, and feature usage
  • Advertising Identifier: With your consent, for personalized ads

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Saving and syncing your game progress across devices
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs
  • Understanding how players use the app
  • Serving relevant advertisements (with your consent)
  • Ensuring fair gameplay (lives reset timing, etc.)

Third-Party Services

Stop It! uses the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Anonymous authentication, cloud storage for game progress, and analytics
  • Data Shared: Anonymous user ID, game progress, device information
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

AdMob (Google)

  • Purpose: Serving optional rewarded video ads
  • Data Shared: Advertising identifier (with consent), device information
  • Privacy Policy: AdMob Privacy Policy

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, we will request your permission before tracking you across apps and websites owned by other companies. This permission is used solely for personalized advertising. You can change your preference at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

Data Retention

We retain your game progress data for as long as you use the app. If you don't use the app for an extended period (typically 2 years), we may delete your data to free up storage.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • We use Firebase's security rules to protect data access
  • No sensitive personal information is collected or stored

Children's Privacy

Stop It! is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses anonymous authentication only and does not require any personal information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we store about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your game progress (you can also delete the app)
  • Opt-Out: Disable ad tracking via iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in any country where Firebase operates. By using Stop It!, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, please contact us:

Email: privacy@gzb-apps.com

Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - EU
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - California, USA
  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) - USA
